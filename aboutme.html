About Me

Have questions? I have answers.

My Mission Statement:

I strive to create positive, tangible experiences to make the most impact on those around me.
Good experiences saves lives.




Where I'm From:

I am from the lovely state of Oregon! I grew up in a large suburb of Portland called Hillsboro. Hillsboro was a great and diverse community that I learned a lot from. Having the wackiness of Portland nearby also sparked my passion for events and creating memorable experiences. I lived in Hillsboro throughout my entire childhood. The first time I moved was to come to Utah for college.

My Passion Project:

My sister has Type 1 Diabetes. It has constantly been a struggle to carry all the supplies and to prevent the insulin from getting too hot to the point where it stops working. Summer vacations and hikes needed careful planning and yet, the insulin still died more than once. My sister's insulin died while she was on her honeymoon. No one should have to worry about that on their honeymoon. To combat this problem, I am designing a bag with a compartment that's temperature is electronically regulated so insulin always stays at the right temperatures. I also have a few ideas on reducing the bulk of supplies, but those will be tricky since the diabetic supplies industry is out to make a profit. I plan on changing the name and establishing a unique brand. I also am in need of someone who would be able to help design the electronically regulated compartment. So if you or someone you know is good with electricity and building/designing things, please contact me!
